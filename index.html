About ERF

Epistemic Research Frameworks (ERF) is an independent research organism dedicated to high-level conceptual, epistemic, and methodological work.

Research

ERF focuses on epistemic structures, ontologies of practice, and frameworks for rigorous, non-noisy inquiry.
